Eulogy to Representation

Photobash

2025

For a brief period of time, every image uploaded to the internet either represented reality directly, or was touched by an artist in some way. Around 2022, that changed, with the advent of AI generated imagery. Since then, it has become increasingly difficult to tell if an image is real or AI generated. This piece seeks to eulogize the era where all pixel data on the internet was in one way or another, "real."
